Troubleshooting Guide

App Not Opening

If the app doesn't open or crashes immediately:

  1. Force close all apps and restart your device
  2. Check if iOS needs updating
  3. Delete and reinstall the app
  4. Ensure you have enough storage space (at least 500MB free)

App is Laggy or Slow

To improve app performance:

  1. Close other running apps
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Clear app cache (delete and reinstall)
  4. Ensure your iOS is up to date
